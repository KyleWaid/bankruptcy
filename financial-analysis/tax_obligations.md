## OWED YEAR (2022) — PENALTY AND INTEREST ANALYSIS

### Federal ($7,585 owed)

**Failure-to-File Penalty (IRC 6651(a)(1)):**
- 5% of unpaid tax per month, max 25%
- 17 months late → 25% max reached
- $7,585 × 25% = $1,896

**Failure-to-Pay Penalty (IRC 6651(a)(2)):**
- 0.5% of unpaid tax per month
- 17 months × 0.5% = 8.5%
- $7,585 × 8.5% = $645

**Combined penalty reduction:** When both apply simultaneously, failure-to-file is reduced by failure-to-pay amount for overlapping months. Net combined rate is 5% per month (not 5.5%).

**Interest (IRC 6601):**
- Compounds daily on unpaid tax + penalties
- Rate varies (IRS adjusts quarterly, typically 7-8% in 2023-2024)
- Estimated ~17 months of interest on ~$7,585 at ~7%: ~$760

**Estimated Federal Total:**

|-----------|-----------------|
| Original tax owed | $7,585 |
| Failure-to-file penalty (25% max) | ~$1,896 |
