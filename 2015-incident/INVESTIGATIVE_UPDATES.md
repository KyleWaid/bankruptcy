## CALIBRATED FRAMING OF THE CASE

Kyle's position, after reflection and objective analysis:

**Kyle believes the bride may not have actively participated in drugging Hillary but believes she has knowledge of what happened that night.**

This is the more defensible framing because:

1. The bride's 2024 text responses read as a knowledgeable witness protecting herself, not necessarily as a perpetrator
2. Group silence around DFSA events is more often composed of bystanders than of active participants
3. A "witness with knowledge" framing is more provable than a "perpetrator" framing
4. Investigators can approach her as a potential witness (with available tools like use immunity) rather than as a suspect

**Recommended framing when speaking to the detective:**

> "I believe the bride has information about what happened that night. She has denied having information. I believe the evidence is more consistent with her having knowledge than with her being unaware. I'm asking investigators to interview her as a potential witness."
