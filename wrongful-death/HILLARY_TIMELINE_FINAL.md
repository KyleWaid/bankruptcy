## CENTRAL FINDING

**Hillary Ann Waid made the decision to die approximately 8 months before her death on March 16, 2026.**

The decision was documented on her phone, dated her 43rd birthday (July 23, 2025). She wrote that this was going to be the last year of her life, that she knew what she had to do, and that she was willing to do it.

This finding fundamentally re-anchors the timeline of responsibility for her death. Events in the final weeks (the 2/17/2026 hospitalization and first attempt; arguments about alcohol access; the rage notes; the March 16 act) were the **execution** of a decision already made. They were not its cause.
